About The Casor Magazine

The CASOR Magazine is a vibrant and inspiring e-magazine created by and for Christian students. It is a pioneering publication heralded by The Academic House of the Christ Ambassadors Students' Outreach (CASOR), ABU- Zaria. The magazine is a platform that aims to provide a diverse range of content that is relevant, grounded in Bible principles and engaging to the Christian student. We are young, vibrant, expectant students, who are far from home and learning anew; meeting people, experiencing success and failure, finding balance and above all knowing God in all of these. This is our story! We want to explore our faith and creativity. It is open to tertiary students, inter denominational student fellowship bodies and youth workers.
